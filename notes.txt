
Architecture 
The website will have two main pages 
● Story home page 
● Collection landing page 
Story Home Page 
- Illustrated brand story journey (parallax plus bee animation) - with option to skip for repeat users 
- Presentation of currently featured collection - reference - https://pin.it/5dYYQZZWO  
- Image+text and link to non-featured collections (this option is for the future when we have more collections) 
Collection landing page 
- Presentation of collection - reference - https://pin.it/5dYYQZZWO   (repeat the same thing from the Story Home page because this is for users who come to the website from ads/purchase links. 
- Each product links out to an individual product page 
- The individual product page has Photos, video, descriptive text e-commerce standard options + add to cart/buy now etc 
- Editorial-style images and text explaining the story behind the collection 
Website Design Notes: 
- The website is to have a storytelling-based layout everywhere - don’t want it to look like a corporate website 
- Product home page and individual pages - bunch these by collection. Every time there is a new collection, it will have a separate set of landing pages and individual product pages. At no point are we going to show all the different collections on one common page 
because each collection has its own language, aesthetic and world.
